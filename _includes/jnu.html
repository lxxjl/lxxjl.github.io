JNU

JNU, short for Jinan University, is a public research and comprehensive university based on Guangzhou. It was founded in 1906 in Nanjing, whose mission is to spread Chinese learning and culture from North to South, and from China overseas. It is the first university in China to recruit foreign students, and is currently a Chinese university with the largest number of international students. The international atmosphere in JNU enables me to gain a global scope, and to make friends around the world.
